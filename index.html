ABOUT US

What Makes our Coffee Special ?

The Coffee Shop has become a daily necessity for local coffee fans. Students and faculty of the University of Tennessee and all of Knoxville can escape to a comfortable place to meet friends and colleagues to study, socialize, read a book and have a great cup of coffee and espresso.

With the growing demand for high-quality gourmet coffee and great service, The Coffee Shop has captured and will continue to capture its core group of repeat coffee lovers.

In addition to our walk-in patrons, we roast, package & distribute to local Knoxville businesses and the surrounding East TN area. Come to one of our coffee shops or visit one of our coffee partners and indulge your taste buds with our coffee sourced from the most tasty beans on earth, selected for a great mixture of fresh and exotic taste!
